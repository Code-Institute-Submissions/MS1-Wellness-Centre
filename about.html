Now a bit about us...

Here at Central Wellness we welcome you to our friendly family! Our mission is to support people with their own personal goals, whether it be weightloss, smoking cessation or having a sense of belonging. The Wellness Centre contains a studio where classes are hosted, a gym facility with personal trainers, changing rooms, showers and toilets, therapy rooms and a communal foyer where people can make friends over a cuppa.

Some Of The Therapies We Have To Offer:
  • 1:1 Yoga and Pilates Sessions
  • Acupunture
  • Reflexology
  • Physiotherapy
  • Talking Therapies
